PodCraft transforms any topic into an engaging interview-style podcast episode — complete with AI-written scripts and natural-sounding speech synthesis.

🎙️ How It Works
1. Enter any topic — from quantum physics to cooking tips
2. AI generates a natural host-and-guest conversation with narrative arc
3. Azure Speech synthesizes the script with two distinct voices
4. Listen instantly or download for later

✨ Key Features

• AI-Written Scripts — Natural dialogue between a curious host and knowledgeable guest, with conversational flow and narrative structure

• Natural Speech — Azure Neural TTS produces studio-quality audio with two distinct voices that sound remarkably human

• Interactive Sessions — Steer the conversation in real time. Pause, ask questions, challenge points, and explore tangents

• Android Auto — Listen to your podcasts on the road with full Android Auto integration

• Dark Mode — Beautiful Material 3 design with automatic dark/light theme support

• Offline Ready — Episodes are cached for listening without an internet connection

🎧 Perfect For
• Learning about new topics during your commute
• Generating educational content
• Exploring ideas in a conversational format
• Content creators looking for podcast inspiration

🔒 Privacy First
PodCraft uses secure JWT authentication with encrypted storage. Your data stays between you and your server. Self-host the backend for complete control.

Built with Jetpack Compose, Media3, and Material 3. Powered by Azure OpenAI and Azure Speech Services.
